Media / Video Editing

Editing story-driven videos for clarity, pace, and audience retention.

My editing work is built around making information easier to watch and easier to remember. I focus on pacing, clean structure, topic clarity, and thumbnail-title alignment so each video feels more intentional.

Featured Projects Contact
Editing Focus

Jump cuts, clean pacing, title-hook alignment, and video structure that supports educational storytelling.

Editing Style

Explainer-driven, hook-first, readable pacing, and topic presentation that keeps attention.

Key Learnings

Retention matters

Strong first lines, faster cuts, and cleaner framing help viewers stay with the topic longer.

Structure improves impact

A video becomes easier to follow when the hook, explanation, and conclusion are clearly separated.

Editing supports message

Good edits are not only visual. They make the information itself easier to understand.
