Tips and Resources

Pick one clear idea per video

Short-form content becomes stronger when the viewer can understand the main point without confusion.

Write the hook after research

Good titles feel sharper when you already know the strongest fact, conflict, or surprise inside the topic.

Study retention patterns

Watch where energy drops in good videos. The answer often comes from structure, pacing, or a weak transition.
